And when we went home, it felt like we’d faced something big and came out stronger. Every time we’re together, whether at festivals, playing games, or just curled up watching movies, you’ve brought so much joy into my life. I’ve seen how hard you work to improve your life, and I admire your determination and your heart. You inspire me in so many ways, and I am so proud of the person you are. I love how you care for the people in your life, how you make me laugh, and how you always make me feel seen, even when we’re far apart. You are truly one of a kind. Thank you for being my person. I can’t wait for the day we’re in the same place again, but until then, I’ll hold onto every memory, every laugh, every promise we’ve made to each other. Here’s to believing in us, and everything we’ll build together. Happy Birthday, my love. I love you more than words can ever say. 🎂🎉
